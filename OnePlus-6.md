# OnePlus 6

## Hardware Support

* Camera:
> Works with both AOSP camera and OP6-specific MGC

* Speaker / Microphone
> Works

* Bluetooth
> Works

* Wifi
> Works

* SIM / Mobile Data / Voice
> Works (tested with one SIM)

* VoLTE
> Not working

* Fingerprint Reader
> Works

* Brightness control
> Works

***
