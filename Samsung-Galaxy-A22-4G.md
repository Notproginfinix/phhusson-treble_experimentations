# Samsung Galaxy A22 4G

## Steps to install
* Flash GSI image with `Fastboot` or [`TWRP`](https://forum.xda-developers.com/t/recovery-unofficial-twrp-for-galaxy-a22-sm-a225f-a225m-android-11-12.4333305/)
